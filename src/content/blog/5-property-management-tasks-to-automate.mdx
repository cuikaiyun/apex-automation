# 5 Property Management Tasks You Should Automate Right Now

If you're still chasing maintenance requests by text, manually sending rent reminders, or copy-pasting tenant info between spreadsheets — you're not alone. But you're also leaving serious time and money on the table.

Here are five property management workflows that are ripe for automation — and what the impact looks like when you actually make the switch.

---

## 1. Rent Reminders & Late Payment Follow-Ups

Sending rent reminders manually is one of those tasks that eats 30 minutes a week without feeling like work — until you realize you've spent 26 hours on it this year. Automated reminder sequences (sent by email or SMS) can go out 5 days before rent is due, on the due date, and the day after with a late fee notice — all without you touching anything.

> 💡 One property manager we worked with reduced late payments by 35% in the first 60 days after automating this workflow.

---

## 2. Maintenance Request Intake & Routing

When a tenant reports a leaky faucet, you need to capture the details, assign the right vendor, confirm the appointment, and follow up when the job is done. Most PMs do this via text thread — which means things fall through the cracks.

A simple intake form connected to your task management system can auto-route requests by type (plumbing → Licensed Plumber Co., HVAC → AirTech), notify the vendor, and send the tenant a confirmation — all automatically.

---

## 3. Lease Renewal Outreach

Lease renewals shouldn't sneak up on you. An automated workflow can identify leases expiring in 90, 60, and 30 days, trigger personalized outreach to tenants with renewal offers, and even generate draft renewal documents for your review. You show up when a decision needs to be made — not to manage the logistics.

---

## 4. Move-In / Move-Out Checklists & Documentation

Move-in and move-out inspections generate a lot of paperwork — and disputes. Automating this means tenants receive a digital checklist, photos are timestamped and stored automatically, and the completed report goes directly into your records. No printing, no scanning, no email chains.

---

## 5. Vacancy Listing Syndication

When a unit turns over, manually posting to Zillow, Apartments.com, Facebook Marketplace, and your own site is tedious. Automation tools can push listings to multiple platforms from a single source of truth — so your vacancy hits the market faster and stays consistent across every channel.

---

## Where Do You Start?

You don't need to automate everything at once. Start with the task that's causing the most pain or taking the most time. For most property managers, that's either rent collection communications or maintenance routing.
